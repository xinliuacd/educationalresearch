## About
I am Dr Xin Liu, Associate Professor and Master’s Supervisor at the College of Teacher Education, Ningbo University. My research sits at the intersection of educational psychology, mathematics education, teacher education, and international comparative education, with a strong focus on large-scale assessment and survey data (PISA, TALIS, TIMSS).

**Research interests**
- Teaching quality and instructional practices
- Student motivation, emotions (e.g., anxiety), and well-being
- Educational equity and digital inclusion
- Teacher professional development and evaluation
- Cross-national and cross-cultural comparison

**Methods**
- SEM and latent variable modelling
- Multilevel models and cross-level interactions
- Measurement invariance and scale validation
- Mediation/moderation and conditional process modelling
- Trend/longitudinal analyses with PV and weights
