About

Hello! I am Dr. Xin Liu, Associate Professor and Master’s Supervisor at the College of Teacher Education, Ningbo University. My research sits at the intersection of educational psychology, mathematics education, teacher education, and international comparative education.

Research interests

  • Teaching quality and instructional practices
  • Student motivation, emotions (especially mathematics anxiety), and well-being
  • Educational equity and digital inclusion
  • Cross-national and cross-cultural comparisons using PISA/TALIS/TIMSS

Methods

  • Structural equation modelling (SEM) and latent variable models
  • Multilevel modelling and cross-level interactions
  • Measurement invariance and scale validation
  • Mediation / moderation (conditional process) models
