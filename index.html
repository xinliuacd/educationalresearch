About

Hello! I am Dr. Xin Liu, an Associate Professor and Master's Supervisor at the College of Teacher Education, Ningbo University. My research focuses on educational psychology, mathematics education, teacher education, and international comparative research using large-scale datasets (e.g., PISA, TALIS, TIMSS).

Methodologically, I work with structural equation modelling, multilevel modelling, measurement invariance, mediation/moderation, and longitudinal designs.

Research interests

  • Teaching quality and instructional practices
  • Student motivation, emotions (e.g., anxiety), and well-being
  • Educational equity & digital inclusion
  • Teacher professional development and evaluation

Methods

  • SEM / latent variable models
  • Multilevel models & cross-level interactions
  • Measurement invariance
  • Trend and longitudinal analyses
